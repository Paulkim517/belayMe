How Does BelayMe work?

Need someone to belay you?

Trying to find someone to make sure you're safe when climbing is hard. It's the crux in life for most climbers. With BelayMe you can post details of your next climb and have a trustworthy climbing partner to belay you or to make sure you don't go missing the in the wild! You'll climb with all levels and meet new people.

Want to make new climbing buddies?

Trying to make new friends in the world is scary but not with BelayMe. Whether you're a veteran looking for new buddies to climb with or a newbie looking for people to teach you, you can use BelayMe to help you in your search. Belay On!
